# Employee Self-Service User Guide — AEC Group HR Super App

Welcome to the **AEC Group HR Super App**. This self-service portal allows you to log daily work, check-in for attendance, apply for leave, submit expense reimbursement bills, update assigned task progress, and send celebratory wishes to colleagues.

---

## 1. Staff Dashboard & Navigation
When you log in with your employee credentials, your dashboard presents your personal attendance metrics, leave balances, and quick-access cards. E.g.:

```
[Login] -> [Main Dashboard] -> Select Self-Service Card (Work Logs / Reimbursement / Tasks / Notifications)
```

### Quick-Access Cards:
- **Work Logs Card**: Submit your daily work status and logged hours.
- **Reimbursement Card**: Submit expense claims and upload scanned bills.
- **Task Management Card**: View tasks assigned to you by managers and update your progress.
- **Notifications Card**: View company announcements and send wishes to coworkers.

---

## 2. Core Employee Workflows

### 2.1 Daily GPS Attendance Check-In
Your attendance is verified using automated GPS geofencing.

```
[Arrive at Workplace] -> Open Dashboard -> System Checks GPS Coordinates (within 100m) -> Click 'Check-In' (Status -> PRESENT)
```

1. Ensure location permissions are enabled on your device.
2. Open the Super App upon arriving at your department center.
3. If your GPS coordinates fall within the 100-meter department radius, click **Check-In**.
4. Your attendance is marked as `PRESENT`. (Note: Arriving past the grace period automatically logs late status).

---

### 2.2 Submitting Expense Reimbursement Claims
Use this workflow to request refunds for official company expenditures.

```
[Dashboard] -> Click 'Reimbursement' Card -> Fill Form & Attach Scanned Bill -> Click 'Submit Claim' (Status -> PENDING)
```

1. Open the **Reimbursement Center**.
2. Under the submission form, enter the expense title, description, and exact amount.
3. Click **Choose File** to upload a PDF or image of your receipt/bill.
4. Click **Submit Claim**.
5. Your request is queued for HR verification (`PENDING`) and subsequent MD approval (`APPROVED`). Track its real-time status in your personal history table.

---

### 2.3 Updating Assigned Task Progress
Keep your managers updated on your daily work assignments.

```
[Dashboard] -> Click 'Task Management' Card -> Locate Task in Table -> Select Status (In Progress / Completed) -> Click 'Update'
```

1. Open the **Task Management** hub.
2. Under **My Assigned Tasks**, review the details and due dates of tasks delegated to you.
3. Use the dropdown menu next to a task to update its progress from `Pending` to `In Progress` or `Completed`.
4. Click **Update**. Your manager instantly sees the updated progress on their tracking report.

---

### 2.4 Sending Congratulatory & Birthday Wishes
Celebrate milestones with your colleagues directly through the app.

```
[Dashboard] -> Click 'Notifications' Card -> Locate Colleague Milestone -> Select Emojis or Type Wish -> Click 'Send Wish'
```

1. Open the **Notifications Center**.
2. Locate birthday or welcome announcements posted on the board.
3. Click the preset quick-emojis (🎂, 🎉, 🥳) or type a personalized note.
4. Click **Send Wish**. Your message is delivered directly to your colleague's internal mailbox!
